My name is Sid. And this (like Ben's) is a place to publish things that I am involved with or interested in. These days that is mostly software - but I still find time for surfing, cross-country rides, reading, writing, and beer. A lot of my recent work involves data visualization. If you have anything to say, feel free to get in touch.

Food Journal is an app I made that allows people to record what they eat in their browser. But unlike other calorie tracking apps, this app tries to be as minimal as possible. Allowing users to use natural language to describe what they are eating. I am currently working on adding mapping, touch functionaility, and a Material Design UI.

I recently started No Forks Given, a Pasta-only food truck in the SF Bay Area. No Forks Given is as the name says pasta served from a food truck with no utensils given - saving space and increasing sunlight probably. They finished on KickStarter and are moving to retail.

Pack ME is a cross-platform app that I released on Android, iOS, and Chrome. My goal was to create a friendly space to remember the things you are packing when you are going away somewhere. Instead of using brain training my app just takes pictures of things that matter most to you when you travel.

Kahasi is an artificial life simulation that I am writing with Python and OpenCL. The simulation is designed to run at scale - to efficiently use as much hardware as you could possibly throw at it.

I'm building Sanjay the IT Guy, a time management game (to the likes of DinerDash) that puts you in the shoes of Sanjay an under-paid IT Guy who has to put out literal and figurative fires in his office. This project is under construction.
