SID GHODKE

My name is Sid. And this is a place to publish things that I am involved with or interested in. These days that is mostly software - but I still find time for surfing, cross-country rides, reading, writing, and beer. A lot of my recent work involves data visualization. If you have anything to say, feel free to get in touch.

Food Journal is unlike other calorie tracking apps, this app tries to be as minimal as possible — allowing users to use natural language to describe what they are eating. I am currently working on adding mapping and a Material Design UI.

I recently started No Forks Given, a Pasta-only food truck in the SF Bay Area. No Forks Given is as the name says pasta served from a food truck with no utensils given - saving space and increasing sunlight probably.

Pack ME is a cross-platform app that I released on Android, iOS, and Chrome. My goal was to create a friendly space to remember the things that matter most to you when you travel.

Kahasi is a SuperH/JCore SoC emulator that I am writing with arm.js and PyPy.js. The simulation is designed to run the full Linux and XWindows sandbox environment right in the browser.

I'm building Sanjay the IT Guy, a time management game (to the likes of DinerDash) that puts you in the shoes of Sanjay an under-paid IT Guy who has to put out literal and figurative fires in his office.

My newest project is Roscida, a tool to move communications among teams into the 21st century. This tool will migrate conversations from Mailers and USENET groups into Discourse community. Users will be able to search previous conversations, create new threads all while using a great web-first User Interface.
