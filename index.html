These days that is mostly software - but I still find time for surfing, cross-country rides, reading, writing, and beer. ~A lot of my recent work involves data visualization.~ If you have anything to say, feel free to get in touch.

Deep Map is an app I made that allows people to blend map layers in their browser like they would blend image layers in Photoshop. This app invites users to explore geographic data to research questions like "Where should I buy a house?" or "Where should I go out tonight?" I am currently working on adding more maps, drag-and-drop layers, and a Material Design UI.

I recently designed Window Pods, a beautiful indoor gardening product. Window Pods are flower pots that attach to your window - saving space and increasing sunlight to plants. They finished on KickStarter and are moving to retail.

UP Brain Trainer is a cross-platform app that I released on Android, iOS, and Chrome. My goal was to create a friendly space to perform N-Back brain training without the pattern-recognition games prevalent in mainstream brain-training apps.

Kahasi is an artificial life simulation that I am writing with Python and OpenCL. The simulation is designed to run at scale - to efficiently use as much hardware as you could possibly throw at it.

I'm building App Engine CMS, an open-source alternative to SquareSpace. It is a Google App Engine boilerplate with a WYSIWYG editor based on html5 contenteditable. This project is under construction.
